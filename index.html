Our Services

We deal in manufacturing and trading of twill tapes, draw cards and other garment accessories, embroidery fabric, Ready to Cut Fabrics Supply, knitted/woven Garments across Pakistan. Specialized in ladies cloth, eastern, western and ethnic dresses.
